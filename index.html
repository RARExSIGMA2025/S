📖 About Us

Empowering Students with Skills that Matter

At Sankalp Computer Classes And IT Classes, we believe in the power of technology to transform lives. Established in Ratanpura, Mau, our coaching center has become a trusted name for aspiring IT professionals and students looking to gain a strong foundation in computers.

Our mission is to deliver practical, result-driven computer education that empowers our students to succeed in the modern digital world.
